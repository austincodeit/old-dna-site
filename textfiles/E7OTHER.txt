In district E7, the majority of Neighborhood complaints are property abatement complaints. For multifamily, structure condition complains lead, and Land-use violations comprise the most of the commercial workload.

The racial makeup of this district differs drastically from the racial makeup of Austin's urban core. The White population in E7 is significantly smaller than in the rest of the city, and the Black population is significantly larger. Black and Hispanic residents are by far the majority in this district. The population of E7 also has a higher percentage of children than in the rest of Austin's Urban Core. The average household size in the district is greater than the Urban Core, while the average persons per acre is less. This reveals that the district has more people per household, while the Urban Core has more multifamily and higher density housing. 

Source:  East MLK Combined Neighborhood Plan
