<p>District E4 lies east of Highway 183 and north of the Colorado River. It includes the Decker Lake area. According to the 2015 American Community Survey conducted by the US Census Bureau, its population is about 15,000 people. About 55% of its 3781 housing units are owner-occupied.</p>

<p>In district E4, the most common Neighborhood complaints are property abatement complaints. For multifamily and commercial, structure condition complaints comprise most of the workload.</p>
